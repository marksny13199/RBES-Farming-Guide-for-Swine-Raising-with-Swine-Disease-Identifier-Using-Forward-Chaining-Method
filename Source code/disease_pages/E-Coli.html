E-Coli

Description

Colibacillosis, or E. coli infection, is one of the major diseases for swine industry which is a typical bacterial disease caused by pathogenic Escherichia coli (E. coli). It mostly causes illness and death in neonatal and recently weaned pigs.

Pangunahin nitong sinasaktan at pinapatay ang mga neonatal at kamakailang naalis na mga baboy.

Cause of the Disease

One of the causes of E-coli is ingesting bacteria in the feces of infected swine mostly this is because of unscheduled feeding or wrong food or feeds that may get them infected and vulnerable in infections.

See Translation...

~ Isa sa mga sanhi ng E-coli ay ang paglunok ng bacteria sa dumi ng mga infected na baboy kadalasan ito ay dahil sa unscheduled feeding o maling pagkain o feeds na maaaring mahawa at madaling mahawa sa impeksyon.

Treatment

Water-soluble antibiotics with multi-vitamins as medication and they should be treated individually to not cause some infections.

See Translation...

~ Mga antibiotic na nalulusaw sa tubig na may mga multi-bitamina bilang gamot at dapat silang tratuhin nang paisa-isa upang hindi magdulot ng ilang impeksiyon.

Prevention

Keep the water source clean and surroundings clean. As for the food or feeds it must be new or latest.
