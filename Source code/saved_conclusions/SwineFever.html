SAVED DISEASE

Swine Fever

Description

Swine Fever is one of the viral diseases that is highly contagious and deadly in both domestic and feral swine of all ages. This cannot be transmitted from pigs to human and it is not food safety issue.

See Translation...

~ Ang Swine Fever ay isa sa mga viral na sakit na lubhang nakakahawa at nakamamatay sa parehong domestic at feral na baboy sa lahat ng edad. Hindi ito maipapasa mula sa baboy patungo sa tao at hindi ito isyu sa kaligtasan ng pagkain

The observed signs and symptoms

Signs and Symptoms of the Disease

Fever or Reddening of eyes, Loss of appetite, and Trembling.

See Translation...

~ Lagnat o Pamumula ng mga mata, Walang ganang kumain, at Panginginig.
Cause of the Disease

The swine fever has a variety of causes such as Reddening of eyes, fever and insect bites (mosquitos).

See Translation...

~ Ang swine fever ay may iba't ibang dahilan tulad ng pamumula ng mata, lagnat at kagat ng insekto (lamok).

Treatment

Injecting Penicillin sulfa will help treat the fever the number of dosage and instruction are already present in the vials.

See Translation...

~ Ang pag-iniksyon ng Penicillin sulfa ay makakatulong sa paggamot sa lagnat ang bilang ng dosis at pagtuturo ay naroroon na sa mga vial.

Prevention

Cleaning the surroundings or area, and always disinfect the area.

See Translation...

~ Paglilinis ng paligid o lugar, at palaging disimpektahin ang lugar.
